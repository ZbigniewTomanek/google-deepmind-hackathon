# Stage 1: Corpus, Diagnostics, and Environment Smoke Check

**Goal**: Validate the shared resources and confirm the current system can ingest,
route, and extract one document on a fresh install.
**Dependencies**: None

---

## Files

- `docs/plans/30-elastic-upper-ontology/resources/test_corpus.md`
- `docs/plans/30-elastic-upper-ontology/resources/commands.md`
- `docs/plans/30-elastic-upper-ontology/resources/queries.md`
- `scripts/manage.sh`

---

## Steps

1. **Review the corpus and command pack**
   - Confirm the 12 documents in `resources/test_corpus.md`
   - Confirm the matching `curl` commands in `resources/commands.md`
   - Confirm the diagnostic queries in `resources/queries.md`

2. **Start from a fresh environment**
   - Run `./scripts/manage.sh stop --all && ./scripts/manage.sh start --fresh`
   - Confirm both services are healthy with `./scripts/manage.sh status`

3. **Verify the seed-domain registry**
   - Run Q1 from `resources/queries.md`
   - Confirm exactly 4 domains exist and all are `seed = true`

4. **Dry-run one ingestion**
   - Ingest Doc 01 from `resources/commands.md`
   - Wait until both `route_episode` and shared-schema `extract_episode` jobs complete via the `procrastinate_jobs` query in `resources/commands.md`

5. **Verify end-to-end behavior**
   - Confirm the personal graph has at least 1 episode for the agent
   - Confirm Q2 shows at least 1 routed episode for `technical_knowledge`
   - Confirm Q3 shows non-zero nodes or edges in `ncx_shared__technical_knowledge`
   - Capture the relevant routing/provisioning logs from `log/agent_actions.log`

---
